People's Tree

People's Tree Pvt Ltd. is an Indian electronic commerce company based in Bengaluru, India. Founded by Ayanava Banerjee, Sudip Banik and Diptayan Dasgupta in 2021-22, the company initially focused on book sales, before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon' s Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal. as of March 2022.
